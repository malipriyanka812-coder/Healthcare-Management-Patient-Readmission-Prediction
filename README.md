# 🏥 Healthcare Management: Patient Readmission Prediction

## 📌 Project Overview

Patient readmission within 30 days of hospital discharge is a major challenge for healthcare providers. Frequent readmissions increase healthcare costs and may indicate gaps in patient care.

This project analyzes patient demographics, medical history, hospital admissions, procedures, medications, and discharge information to identify factors associated with patient readmission. The insights help healthcare providers recognize high-risk patients and support better clinical decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Healthcare facilities need to identify patients who are at high risk of being readmitted within 30 days after discharge.

The objective of this project is to analyze patient data and uncover patterns that help:

* Predict high-risk patients
* Reduce hospital readmission rates
* Improve patient outcomes
* Support proactive healthcare planning

## 📈 Key Insights

* Readmission rates vary across different age groups.
* Elderly patients show a higher likelihood of readmission.
* Certain diagnosis categories contribute more to repeat hospital visits.
* Longer hospital stays are associated with higher readmission risk.
* Medication count and laboratory procedures provide valuable indicators of patient complexity.
